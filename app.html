04Seasonal Tip · Detail
09:41● ● ▮
Seasonal Tip
Published Apr 22

Prune your Robusta before the rains

AG
Achieng Grace·Senior Agronomist·3 min read

The short rains are a fortnight away in Mpigi, Butambala and Gomba. Pruning your Robusta trees now opens the canopy so next month's blossoms set into bigger, cleaner cherries.

What to cut: low branches touching the ground, suckers growing from the stump, and cross-branches shading the middle of the tree.

What to leave: healthy bearing branches from last season and new shoots angled upward.

Collect all cuttings and burn them away from the field — this stops coffee leaf rust overwintering on shed leaves.
