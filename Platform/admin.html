Products & Shop

Manage your product catalogue and stock levels.

Produce Prices

Monthly guide prices shown to farmers in the mobile app under "Sell My Produce". Updated by the admin team from market surveys.

Traceability · Coffee & Hass Avocado

Each batch is tracked from farmer harvest through to export. Farmers look up their Batch ID in the app; exporters can share the same data with importers.

Orders

Full order lifecycle across farmer, agent and on-behalf orders. Diaspora orders are handled separately under "Diaspora Orders".

Diaspora Orders · Visa payments

Orders paid in USD by Ugandan diaspora via Stripe, delivered in Uganda through our outlets. Recipients collect with a pickup code.

FX rate reminder. The rate 1 USD = UGX 3,800 is set manually in Settings → FX Rate. Orders are invoiced at the rate at time of payment, not today's.

For Hire Providers

Tractors, water pumps, threshers, and transport available for hire across Uganda.

Users & Roles

Invite your team, assign roles, and control exactly what each person can see and change. Only Managing Directors can edit the permission matrix.

Invite your first team member
Pick from 5 preset roles (MD, Sales Manager, IT Admin, Senior Agronomist, Outlet Clerk) or build a custom role with granular permissions. Invitees get an email and set their own OTP.

Agents & Leaderboard

Field Officers
